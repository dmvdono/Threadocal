FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Quick answers for shoppers, local clothing brands, fees, pickup, and accounts.

What is Threadocal?

A discovery marketplace for independent clothing brands, searchable by ZIP code, city, category, and pickup radius.

Is Threadocal only local?

The goal is national coverage, while still helping shoppers find brands near them.

Can brands join free?

Yes. The concept includes free brand signup with optional paid promotional services.

Does Threadocal take a fee?

The prototype includes a small Threadocal purchase fee concept and future negotiable brand royalty agreements.

Can I chat about pickup?

That is planned for the customer side, so shoppers can coordinate local pickup with brands.

Will there be an app?

Yes, a mobile app is part of the future development plan.

About Us

Built for local clothing culture.

Threadocal exists to help independent clothing brands get discovered without being buried under massive marketplaces.

Our Mission

Make it easier for shoppers to discover unique clothing brands in their city and across the country, while giving small brands tools to grow.

Audience

Designed for shoppers ages 16–40 who care about local culture, originality, and supporting small businesses.

Vision

At least one clothing brand discoverable within every 30-mile radius across America.

Difference

Threadocal focuses on clothing brands, not every product category like Amazon or eBay.
